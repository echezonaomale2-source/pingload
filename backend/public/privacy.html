1. Introduction

Pingload ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Pingload mobile application and related services at pingload.top. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our fintech platform for wallet funding, airtime, data, electricity, TV subscriptions, and related services.

By creating an account or using Pingload, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Account information: full name, email address, phone number, password (stored as a secure hash), profile photo (optional), and referral code.
  • Identity and KYC data: government-issued identification, address, and documents you submit for verification when required.
  • Transaction data: purchase history, wallet top-ups, transfers, service type (airtime, data, electricity, TV, etc.), amounts, status, and timestamps.
  • Device and technical data: device type, operating system, app version, push notification tokens, IP address, and diagnostic logs used to maintain service reliability.
  • Security data: transaction PIN (stored encrypted), biometric login preferences (stored on your device where supported), and login activity.
  • Communications: messages you send to customer support, feedback, and notification preferences.

3. How we use your information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Process wallet funding, bill payments, VTU purchases, and peer transfers.
  • Send transaction confirmations, security alerts, and service notifications.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests.
  • Improve our products, fix errors, and provide customer support.
  • Send promotional communications where you have opted in (you may opt out at any time).

4. Payment information

Wallet funding is processed through Paystack, a licensed payment processor. When you fund your wallet, payment card or bank details are entered directly on Paystack's secure checkout — Pingload does not store your full card number or CVV on our servers.

We retain payment references, transaction amounts, status, and timestamps to reconcile your wallet balance, process refunds where applicable, and resolve disputes.

5. Wallet information

Your Pingload wallet balance and transaction ledger are stored securely in our systems. We use this information to:

  • Display your balance and transaction history in the app.
  • Deduct funds when you purchase services or send transfers.
  • Credit your wallet after successful Paystack payments.
  • Investigate failed, reversed, or disputed transactions.

Wallet adjustments by authorized administrators are logged for audit and compliance purposes.

6. Cookies and analytics

The Pingload mobile app does not use browser cookies. Our website and admin dashboard may use essential session storage or similar technologies required for authentication and security.

We may collect anonymized usage analytics (such as feature usage and crash reports) to improve app stability and performance. You can manage notification and certain data preferences in the app Settings screen.

7. Third-party services

We share limited data with trusted partners only as needed to deliver our services:

  • Paystack — payment processing and wallet funding verification.
  • VTpass — airtime, data, electricity, TV, and other bill payment fulfillment.
  • Firebase (Google) — push notifications and device messaging infrastructure.
  • Termii — SMS and email OTP delivery for registration and account recovery.
  • Cloud hosting providers — secure storage and operation of our API and databases.

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and applicable data protection agreements. We require them to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

8. Data security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords, protected API authentication (JWT), rate limiting, and access controls for administrative systems.

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please use a strong password, enable biometric login where available, and never share your transaction PIN.

9. Your rights

Depending on applicable law (including the Nigeria Data Protection Act), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for optional processing such as promotional messages.
  • Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.

10. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Transaction records may be retained for regulatory and audit purposes.

11. Children

Pingload is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest version. Material changes will be communicated through the app or by email where appropriate.
